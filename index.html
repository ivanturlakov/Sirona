INTEGRATED CHAIRSIDE RESTORATIONS

Consisting of a CEREC Omnicam with camera tray, the CEREC AI (Acquisition Integrated) system integrates into the TENEO treatment unit via a separate arm, and a PC with 24" or 19" monitor positioned behind the patient's head. The patient—and you, too—can follow everything directly on the treatment unit monitor or on the tablet supplied with the CEREC AI system. This is an ideal solution for practices that want to offer CEREC restorations in conjunction with the TENEO treatment center.
