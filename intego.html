WORKFLOW INTEGRATION

Your workday is complex and places many different demands on the treatment center you choose. INTEGO provides a choice between two models with flexible configurations to meet your needs.

Intego TS Intego CS
What do these two options come with?

BOTH MODELS OFFER THE FOLLOWING:

  • EasyTouch user interface allowing complete control of the instrument in use, with a screen showing you what you need when you need it.
  • USB port to connect Schick sensors and take x-rays images with the optional HELIODENTPLUS intraoral source.
  • SIDEXIS 4 integration with direct control through the EasyTouch touch screen.
  • 22" medical grade monitor that allows you to access intraoral and x-ray images, software and planning views, media player files, and PowerPoint presentations directly at the treatment center.
  • Endodontic function that allows you to use your file systems with the INTEGO's interface, EasyTouch and the ENDO 6.1L attachment.
  • A network interface at the patient chair and a USB port in the dentist or assistant element allow you to integrate new functions and network your practice. Troubleshooting by your service technician is also much easier.
