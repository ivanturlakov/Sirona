Integration

Sirona treatment centers are designed to follow your lead, so no matter what breadth of services you provide now or plan to in the future—such as implantology, endodontics or CEREC—your potential for growth is virtually unlimited.

INTEGRATED ENDODONTICS

Makes tabletop units a thing of the past and, along with precise torque control, offers an ApexLocator for the highest degree of clinical certainty.

View Practice

INTEGRATED IMPLANTOLOGY

With the integrated implantology function, you can set the speed, torque, and flow rate of the sterile cooling fluid individually to suit your workflow.

View Practice

CHAIRSIDE CAD/CAM

The CEREC AC, AF, or AI systems are all great CAD/CAM solutions that can be integrated with our Transcendental treatment centers.

EASE OF USE

Every Sirona treatment center features the EasyTouch touchscreen, providing improved feeling of touch and customizable programming for the integration of new features at any time.

INTEGRATED X-RAY

The HELIODENTPLUS x-ray tube head fits into every practice and can be mounted on the wall, ceiling, or as a mobile unit.

INTEGRATED 3D IMAGING

Sirona 3D imaging systems readily integrate with Sirona treatment centers, optimizing your workflows.

CONNECTIVITY

You can view your entire network through the monitor, control SIDEXIS 4, Windows Media Player, PowerPoint and more via the EasyTouch Touchpad, and receive new software, firmware and system updates via the network interface.
